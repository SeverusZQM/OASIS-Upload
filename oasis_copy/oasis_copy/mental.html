Have you been hit by
these common mental issues
in the workplace?

Worry that you are not doing well enough at work and being afraid of being criticized and blamed.

Establish a correct career concept, accept your own shortcomings, and work hard to improve. At the same time, communicate with colleagues and superiors to seek support and advice.

Busy work, lack of communication and interaction with colleagues and superiors.

Try to actively establish connections with colleagues and superiors, participate in team activities, and share ideas and experiences. You can also expand your social circle by joining interest groups, participating in training, etc.

Too much work pressure, feeling unbearable.

Learn to allocate time reasonably and avoid over-commitment. Seek help and support from colleagues and superiors in a timely manner. At the same time, do proper exercise and rest to maintain physical and mental health.

Long-term repetitive work leads to depression, fatigue and irritability.

Try to find freshness and fun in work, learn new skills and methods. At the same time, take proper rest and relaxation, such as music, deep breathing, etc.
